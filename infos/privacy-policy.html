Collecting Data

The only personal data collected and stored in the DB is the device ID. It's used to either login or create an account if this ID is not referred to. This ID is not encrypted or hashed. All other data, which are the topics and cards made, are also stored in the DB and are not encrypted or hashed.

Deleting Data

The app lets you delete your cards, topics, or account. Please click this link to follow the procedure: https://studycardapi.com/infos/delete-datas.html
